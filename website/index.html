About Us

LivingWater Island Hopping was founded by Wilson Lapinig, a native of Gilutongan Island, together with Billy, a traveler from the United States who fell in love with Cebu's coastal charm.

Their shared vision was to create a tour company that not only showcases the natural beauty of the Visayan Sea but also uplifts the local community through sustainable tourism.

Built on local knowledge and a deep passion for the sea, LivingWater Island Hopping is dedicated to providing authentic experiences while supporting island communities and promoting marine conservation. Every trip is designed to be safe, memorable, and meaningful ‚Äî connecting guests with the true spirit of Cebu.
